Claiming Your Airdrop
When we relaunched the WhiteRabbit token, we created a system that would allow old holders with over ~9.7M tokens at time of holding to retrieve their airdropped tokens 1:1 with the new stealth launch.
In order to claim your airdrop, you must follow these steps:
  1. 1.
    Go to Snowtrace page for our airdrop delivery contract
  2. 2.
    Click on the "Contract" tab (there will be a small green circle with a checkmark next to it)
  3. 3.
    Click on the "Write Contract" tab that showed up
  4. 4.
    Click on the "Connect to Web3" link with a red circle to the left of it - this will prompt you to connect your MetaMask or your WalletConnect wallet to Snowtrace
  5. 5.
    Find the section that says "2. claim", and click on the blue "Write" button
  6. 6.
    Confirm the transaction in your Metamask popup / WalletConnect app
If you are eligible for an airdrop, this process will complete successfully. If you get an out of gas error or the transaction fails, you are not eligible.
